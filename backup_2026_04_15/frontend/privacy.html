Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to TitanNetwork. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy notice, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us.

2. Information We Collect

When you visit our website or play on our Minecraft server, we may collect certain information, including:

  • Account Information: Your Minecraft username, UUID, and email address when you register.
  • Log Data: Your IP address, access times, and activities on the server.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies to keep you logged into the website.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our server and website.
  • To improve, personalize, and expand our community features.
  • To understand and analyze how you use our services.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our rules.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect the security of any personal information we process. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, such as the right to access, correct, or delete your information. You can manage your account details through the user profile menu.

6. Third-Party Services

We use Firebase for authentication and database management. Your data is handled according to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not sell or share your data with other third parties for marketing purposes.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this policy, you may contact the server administrators on our official Discord server.
